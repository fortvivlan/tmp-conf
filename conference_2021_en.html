TMP 2021

Description

The eleventh thematic conference of the "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" series, TMP 2021, was held in Moscow on October 13-15, 2021.

The conference was jointly organized by the Pushkin State Russian Language Institute, the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, and Lomonosov Moscow State University.

In 2021, the conference was held via Zoom.

Important Dates

  • July 25, 2021: abstract submission deadline
  • August 25, 2021: notification of selection results
  • October 13-15, 2021: conference

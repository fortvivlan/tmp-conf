TMP 2021 Invited Speakers

Iliyana Krapova & Guglielmo Cinque

Talk: Second-position clitics, adverbal clitics and V2
Presentation

Affiliation: Università Ca' Foscari Venezia

Iliyana Krapova

Current Position: Associate Professor, Linguistic and Cultural Studies

Research Interests: syntax, comparative linguistics, Slavic linguistics, Balkan linguistics.

Education:

  • Studied Classical languages and English at Sofia University "Sv. Kliment Ohridski"
  • 1996: PhD in General and Comparative Linguistics, University of Plovdiv "Paisij Hilendarski"

Career:

  • Since 2005: Associate Professor at Università Ca' Foscari Venezia
  • Has taught at more than 20 universities worldwide
  • 1994-1995: Fulbright research scholar at University of Massachusetts

Specialization: comparative syntax of (South) Slavic and Balkan languages, Bulgarian linguistics and dialectology.

Guglielmo Cinque

Current Position: Professor Emeritus (since 2019)

Research Interests: linguistic theory, syntax, Romance linguistics, linguistic typology.

Biography: Born in La Spezia in 1948. Attended schools in Venice and the university (Classics and Modern Philology) in Padua, where he graduated in 1971.

Education:

  • 1971: graduated from University of Padua (Classics and Modern Philology)
  • 1973: M.A. in Linguistics from University of California, Berkeley

Career:

  • Since 1976: various positions at Ca' Foscari University, Venice
  • Since 1981: full professor
  • Dean of Faculty of Foreign Languages and Literatures (1999-2002)
  • Chair of Department of Language Sciences (2002-2008)

International Recognition: Honorary Professor of University of Bucharest (since 2006), Honorary member of Linguistic Society of America (since 2010), elected member of Academia Europaea (2012).

TMP 2025 Invited Speakers

Igor Aleksandrovich Mel'čuk

Talk: Phrases of the type (1) Mne est' gde spat' and of the type (2) Mne negde spat'
Handout

Affiliation: University of Montreal

Position: Honorary Doctor of the Institute of Linguistics RAS, Professor Emeritus

Education and Career:

  • 1932: Born October 19 in Odessa
  • 1956: Graduated from the Spanish Department, Faculty of Philology, Moscow State University
  • 1956-1976: Worked at the Institute of Linguistics, USSR Academy of Sciences
  • 1977-2009: Professor at the Department of Linguistics and Translation, University of Montreal

Research Interests: Meaning-Text theory, machine translation, syntactic analysis, lexicography, formal linguistic models

Memberships: Member of the Paris Linguistic Society (1974), American Linguistic Society (1979), European Linguistic Society (1983), Royal Society of Canada (1994)

Valentin Yurievich Gusev

Talk: 1st person singular imperative: semantics, typology and usage

Affiliation: Institute of Linguistics RAS / State Institute of Russian Language

Position: Ph.D., Senior Research Fellow at the Institute of Linguistics RAS, Typology Department

Education and Career:

  • Graduated from the Faculty of Theoretical and Applied Linguistics, Russian State University for the Humanities
  • Working at the Institute of Linguistics since July 1995

Research Interests: grammatical typology, history and synchronic description of Samoyedic languages, history of languages and peoples of Siberia

Yury Aleksandrovich Lander

Talk: Parts-of-speech distinctions and head morphology

Affiliation: HSE University

Position: Ph.D., Research Fellow at the Institute of Oriental Studies RAS, Head of the Bachelor's Program "Fundamental and Computational Linguistics" at HSE University

Education and Career:

  • 1999: Graduated from the Faculty of Theoretical and Applied Linguistics, Russian State University for the Humanities
  • Since 1999: Institute of Oriental Studies RAS
  • Since 2008: Teaching at RSUH
  • Since 2012: HSE University
  • 2012: Ph.D. in Philological Sciences

Research Interests: syntactic and lexical typology, Austronesian languages, Caucasian languages, fieldwork on language documentation (Adyghe, Kabardian-Circassian, Udi and Dargwa languages)

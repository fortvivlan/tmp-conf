TMP 2020

Description

The tenth thematic conference in the series "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters", TMP 2020, was held in Moscow on October 7-9, 2020.

The conference was jointly organized by the Pushkin State Russian Language Institute, the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, and Lomonosov Moscow State University.

In 2020, the conference was held online via Zoom.

  • Mark Baker (Rutgers University, USA)

2013 Conference

About the Conference

The 3rd conference in the series "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" jointly organized by the Institute of Modern Linguistic Research of the Moscow State Pedagogical University and the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences was held in Moscow on 16-18 October 2013.

The conference focused on issues of cross-linguistic diversity and unity in morphology, syntax and their interfaces approached from a variety of theoretical perspectives, formalist and functionalist alike.

For the first time, the TMP conference hosted a special section for undergraduate and doctoral students — GeNSLing 2013 ("The 1st Conference on General, Slavic and Nordic Linguistics for Undergraduate and Doctoral Students"). This landmark event marked the beginning of a tradition of engaging young researchers in the conference series.

GeNSLing 2013

For the first time in the history of the "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" conferences, a special section for young researchers was held — GeNSLing 2013 ("The 1st Conference on General, Slavic and Nordic Linguistics for Undergraduate and Doctoral Students").

This initiative was aimed at:

  • Supporting young researchers in the field of linguistics
  • Creating a platform for presenting undergraduate and doctoral research
  • Integrating the educational process with cutting-edge scientific research
  • Developing international cooperation in linguistics

GeNSLing became an important tradition that continued in subsequent conferences in the series.

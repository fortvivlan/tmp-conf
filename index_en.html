Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters

Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters (TMP) is one of the leading annual conferences on typology and grammar theory in Russia, held since 2011. The conference discusses current issues in typological research and grammatical theory, presents new research results, and creates a platform for professional interaction between specialists from different schools and fields.

TMP 2023

Description

The thirteenth thematic conference of the "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" series, TMP 2023, was held in Moscow on October 11-12, 2023.

The conference was held in-person at the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences (Bolshoy Kislovsky Lane 1, Building 1).

The conference was jointly organized by the Pushkin State Russian Language Institute, the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, and Lomonosov Moscow State University.

Important Dates

  • April 16, 2023: abstract submission deadline
  • May 31, 2023: notification of selection results
  • October 11-12, 2023: conference

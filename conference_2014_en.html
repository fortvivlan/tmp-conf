About the Conference

The 4th conference in the series "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" jointly organized by the Institute of Modern Linguistic Research of the Moscow State Pedagogical University and the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences was held in Moscow on 15-17 October 2014.

The conference was held at the Moscow State University for the Humanities named after M.A. Sholokhov (SMSUH).

The conference focused on issues of cross-linguistic diversity and unity in morphology, syntax and their interfaces approached from a variety of theoretical perspectives, formalist and functionalist alike.

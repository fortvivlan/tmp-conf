TMP 2019

Description

The ninth thematic conference in the series "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters", TMP 2019, was held in Moscow on October 16-18, 2019.

The conference was jointly organized by the Pushkin State Russian Language Institute, the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, and Lomonosov Moscow State University.

Invited Speakers

  • Maria Polinsky (University of Maryland and Harvard University, USA)
  • Martin Haspelmath (Max Planck Institute and Leipzig University, Germany)

Important Dates

  • June 15, 2019: deadline for abstract submission
  • July 31, 2019: notification of acceptance
  • October 16-18, 2019: conference

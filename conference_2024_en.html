TMP 2024

The fourteenth thematic conference of the "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" series, TMP 2024, took place in Moscow on October 17-18, 2024.

The conference was held in hybrid format at the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences (Bolshoy Kislovsky Lane 1, Building 1) and using the Zoom platform.

The conference was jointly organized by the Pushkin State Russian Language Institute, the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, and Lomonosov Moscow State University.

Plenary Speakers

  • Pavel Rudnev
  • Elena Petrukhina

2012 Conference

About the Conference

The 2nd conference in the series "Typology of Morphosyntactic Parameters" was organized and hosted jointly by the Moscow State University for the Humanities named after M.A. Sholokhov (SMSUH) and the Institute of Linguistics of the Russian Academy of Sciences in Moscow on November 14-16, 2012.

The conference focused on issues of cross-linguistic diversity and unity in morphology, syntax and their interfaces approached from a variety of theoretical perspectives, formalist and functionalist alike.

Building on the success of the inaugural 2011 conference, the second conference expanded the format to a three-day event with a rich program including plenary talks by distinguished scholars and thematic sessions on key problems in linguistic typology.

Organizing Institutions

  • Moscow State University for the Humanities named after M.A. Sholokhov (SMSUH) — a leading pedagogical university in Russia with rich traditions in linguistic research
